# AI instruction 8

## Organization currency

It must be fixed by a list (dropdown autocomplete) of ISO 4217 currency codes (3 chars) and must be the following only in this order:

* `EUR`
* `USD`
* `GBP`
* `CAD`
* `CHF`
* `CNY`
* `HKD`
* `JPY`
* `TND`
* `DZD`
* `MAD`
* `TRY`
* `EGP`
* `SAR`
* `AED`
* `QAR`
* `AUD`

By default it's `EUR` for every organization.
